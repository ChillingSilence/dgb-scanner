There are two kinds of wallets available. For starters a Simplified Payment Verification (SPV) wallet is recommended.

Simplified Payment Verification (SPV) Wallet

SPV wallets are fast, secure and reliable. During the setup you will generate a secret seed phrase. This phrase is synonymous with a prive key and must kept securely at anytime.

Electrum Vertcoin Wallet

Full Node Wallet

A full node wallet validates every block and transaction. Furthermore a node distributes and secures the Vertcoin network. The initial synchronization can take some time but during this period you can already start mining.

Nvidia Vertcoin Miner

Selecting a P2Pool is recommended. All P2Pool work together as decentralised mining pool and you can switch pools at anytime without losing any Vertcoin.

You should select a pool that is geographically near to your location, has a high uptime and low ping. To support decentralization a pool with a low miner count and hashrate should be prioritized. To remove trust in any third party you can also host your own P2Pool.
